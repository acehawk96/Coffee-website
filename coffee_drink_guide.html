There are so many diffent ways to enjoy coffee from iced coffee to cappuccino. Here's a guide for ambarking on your caffinated journey!

  • Iced

    Essentially a beverage chilled with ice and comes as either a latte or cold-brew coffee.

  • Coffee

    More of a familar drink consisting of hot brewed coffee.

  • Latte

    Consists of Espresso and steamed milk.

  • Mocha

    Simiilar to a latte constisting of espresso and steamed milk but also includes a chocolatly flavoring.

  • Cappuccino

    Consists of espresso, hot milk and topped with steamed milk foam.

  • Americano

    Consists of simply espresso and hotwater which is great for a quick caffine boost.
