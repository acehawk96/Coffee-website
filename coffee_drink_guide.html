There are so many different ways to enjoy coffee from iced coffee to cappuccino. Here's a guide for embarking on your caffeinated journey!

  • Iced

    Essentially a beverage chilled with ice and comes as either a latte or cold-brew coffee.

  • Coffee

    More of a familiar drink consisting of hot brewed coffee.

  • Latte

    Consists of Espresso and steamed milk.

  • Mocha

    Similar to a latte consisting of espresso and steamed milk but also includes a chocolaty flavoring.

  • Cappuccino

    Consists of espresso, hot milk and topped with steamed milk foam.

  • Americano

    Consists of simply espresso and hot water which is great for a quick caffeine boost.
